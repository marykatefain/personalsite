About Me

I am a tech-savy activist with a dream to make the world a better place for all. I design and create original projects which help people and inspire empathy and action. My diverse range of experiences in neuroscience research, grass-roots activism, non-profit marketing, and web design and development give me a uniquely wide base of skills from which to pull. In my spare time I organize Direct Action Everywhere's Philadelphia chapter, the Philly Animal Rights Coalition, and a burgeoning vegan housing co-op.
